Bio

PINKFONG is a children's entertainment company established in 2010. It is a subsidiary of SmartStudy Co. Ltd, based in South Korea. It specializes in creating educational content for kids. Their videos are posted on their YouTube channel. The videos star Pinkfong, a pink fox who "loves music more than anything", and is the prince of the planet Staria.
